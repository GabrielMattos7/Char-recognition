Rudy Alejandro Cardozo Fernndez (born 14 February 1990) is a Bolivian footballer who plays for The Strongest and the Bolivia national team as a midfielder. Born in Tarija, Cardozo was a Club Bolvar youth graduate. In the 2008 summer he moved to Israel second division side Ironi Ramat HaSharon, making his senior debuts for the latter in the campaign. In July 2009 Cardozo returned to Bolvar, but only appeared rarely for the club during his first year.
