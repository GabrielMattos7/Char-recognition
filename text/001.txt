Nasirdin Isanovich Isanov (Russian Kyrgyz ( ) , romanizedNasirdin Isanovich (Isan uulu) Isanov) 7 November 1943 29 November 1991) was a Kyrgyz politician who served as the first Prime Minister of Kyrgyzstan from 30 August 1991 to 29 November 1991.1 In 1966 he graduated from the Moscow Engineering and Construction Institute, and for several years he worked in the construction industry. After joining the Communist Party in 1969, he rose through party structures, becoming first secretary of the Osh regional Komsomol.
